I’m JESUS TROCONIZ

Telecommunications expert with a degree in Electronic Engineering, and currently Network Manager in South Cone, LATAM. I’m always seeking to improve as person as well as all what is around me. I come from Venezuela nationalized Argentinian, living in Buenos Aires since 2015.

My experience in the field began in Venezuela where I had the opportunity to work for different projects in which I traveled around the country and got my first contact with a diverse networks and technologies. This gave me a wide perspective of the telecommunication world from Layer 1 to 8 (if you know what I’m saying).

Thanks to the knowledges I obtained from two of the best companies in Argentina, I have developed leadership, team-work and effective communication skills as well as an ability to work in highly demanding environments.

During my free time I tend to socialize with my friends, in fact, I’m known as the best Latin dancer. I continue practicing Karate since I was a kid, and I include cycling in my daily routines. Finally, I never stop studying with the deep desire to be always better, to know more and to be out from my comfort zone.
